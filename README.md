## The CDN Thing Actually Matters

Most hosts offer CDN as a paid add-on or wave it around as a marketing term that amounts to very little. SpeedyPage's CDN is powered by Bunny.net — a real CDN with 121+ points of presence worldwide. The Bunny.net Optimiser also handles CSS/JS minification, WebP image conversion, and image resizing on the fly.

In their own benchmarks (using the K6.io methodology from WPHostingBenchmarks.com under 1,000 concurrent users), SpeedyPage handles about 976 requests per second against a standard WordPress install — compared to 480 for 20i and 440 for SiteGround. Take vendor benchmarks with appropriate skepticism, but it's at least in the right ballpark given the hardware they're running.
